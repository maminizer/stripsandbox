About Us

Welcome at

Expirium Events

We operate in two different areas: On the one hand, we produce our own experiences - and we do this with a dedicated in-house team, supported by a global network of creatives and technical service providers. On the other, we book our own experiences and those of partner producers into suitable emerging markets all over the world, working with promoters, museums and other cultural institutions. We also deliver content to shopping malls, hotel resorts, cruiseships and other leisure entrepreneurs.

Ensuring the feasible mobility of our productions, we strongly believe that there is a sublime connection between the mastery of logistics and the creation of astonishment. We are here to serve to the needs of our markets and to the ardor of our target crowds.

Events

We consider events a form of contemporary mass seduction

Our passion is to create excitement. We want to wow and surprise. We turn consumers into ambassadors. We believe in the impact of impression and the power of experience. We consider events a form of contemporary mass seduction.

Differentiated brand experiences drive consumer choice. Being offered a unique experience connects the consumer with the brand. A key message, delivered in a truly unique way remains unforgettable. It’s the experience, garnered from experiential marketing tactics that ties consumers to a brand.

We use our experience and skills in show creation as an interface for developing unique content for events. Our handwriting is characterized by precision and style confidence.

We make dreams come true, and we do it with professionalism and prudence. Our clients can always rely on our knowledge of their needs and our careful and diligent handling of their most important assets.

Product Launches, Presentations, Roadshows, Receptions, Conferences – we deliver the tailormade content. We attach great importance to uniqueness and excitement, opulence and information. For us, imagery and storytelling are always intertwined. We implement marketing goals in a memorable and sustainable way.
